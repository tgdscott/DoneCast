# Quick Guide: Editing the Front Page
**For Non-Technical Users**

## What You Can Do

You can edit **customer testimonials** and **frequently asked questions** that appear on the bottom of the podcastplusplus.com homepage.

## How to Access the Editor

1. **Log in** to podcastplusplus.com with your admin account
2. Go to the **Admin Dashboard** (there should be an admin link in the menu)
3. In the sidebar, click **"Front Page Content"**

## What You'll See

### Blue Information Box
At the top, you'll see a blue box that explains:
- ‚úÖ What you CAN edit (reviews and FAQs)
- üîí What you CANNOT edit (requires developer)

**Important:** If you want to change something that's locked (like the main headline or feature descriptions), you'll need to ask the developer.

## Editing Customer Testimonials

### Section Headers
- **Section Heading:** The title above all testimonials (e.g., "What Our Users Say")
- **Rating Summary:** The overall rating displayed (e.g., "4.9/5 from 2,847 reviews")

### Individual Testimonials
Each testimonial card has:
- **Customer Quote:** What the customer said (required)
- **Customer Name:** Their name (required)
- **Role/Context:** Their job or how long they've used the product (optional)
- **Avatar URL:** Link to their photo (optional - leave blank to show initials)
- **Star Rating:** Number of stars to display (0-5)

### Adding/Removing Testimonials
- Click **"+ Add Testimonial"** at the bottom to add more
- Click **"Remove"** on any testimonial to delete it (must have at least 1)

## Editing FAQs

### Section Headers
- **Section Heading:** The title above all FAQs (e.g., "Frequently Asked Questions")
- **Section Subheading:** Subtitle text (e.g., "Everything you need to know")

### Individual FAQs
Each FAQ has:
- **Question:** The question users ask (required)
- **Answer:** Your response (required)

### Adding/Removing FAQs
- Click **"+ Add FAQ"** at the bottom to add more
- Click **"Remove"** on any FAQ to delete it (must have at least 1)

## Saving Your Changes

1. After making edits, click **"üíæ Save changes"** at the top
2. You'll see a green success message
3. Your changes are **immediately live** on podcastplusplus.com
4. Open the website in a new private/incognito window to see your changes

## Other Buttons

- **"Discard changes"** - Undo all unsaved edits
- **"Reset to defaults"** - Restore the original default content

## Tips

1. **Preview your changes:** Save them, then visit podcastplusplus.com in a private browser window
2. **Be specific:** Good testimonials include the customer's name, what they do, and specific results
3. **Keep FAQs clear:** Write questions the way customers would ask them
4. **Use real data:** If you claim "4.9/5 from 2,847 reviews", make sure that's accurate!

If you can't figure something out or need to change a locked section:
1. Take a screenshot of what you want to change
2. Ask the developer
3. They can either help you or make the change in the code
